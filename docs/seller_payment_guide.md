## How Payments Work

When a buyer purchases your product on HEKKA MARKET, the payment process follows these steps:

1. **Purchase**: Buyer completes checkout through our secure payment system powered by Lemon Squeezy.

2. **Transaction Processing**: The payment is processed immediately, and the buyer receives access to your digital product.

3. **Payment Holding Period**: Funds are held in your HEKKA MARKET balance for a security period (see [Getting Paid](#getting-paid) for details).

4. **Payout**: After the holding period, funds are automatically transferred to your connected bank account or PayPal according to your payout schedule.

## Getting Paid

### Payout Methods

HEKKA MARKET supports two primary payout methods:

1. **Direct Bank Transfer** (recommended)
   - Available in 50+ countries
   - No additional fees
   - 2-5 business days to process

2. **PayPal**
   - Available worldwide
   - 2% additional fee
   - 1-2 business days to process

### Payout Schedule

You can choose your preferred payout schedule:

| Schedule | Minimum Balance | Availability |
|----------|----------------|--------------|
| Weekly | $50 | All sellers |
| Daily | $100 | Premium Sellers only |
| Manual | $100 | Available on request |

### Holding Period

For security and fraud prevention, HEKKA MARKET implements a holding period before releasing funds:

| Seller Status | Holding Period |
|---------------|----------------|
| New Sellers (first 3 months) | 14 days |
| Established Sellers | 7 days |
| Premium Sellers | 3 days |

### Setting Up Your Payout Method

1. Go to your Seller Dashboard
2. Navigate to "Payments" → "Payout Settings"
3. Choose your preferred payout method
4. Complete the required verification steps
5. Set your preferred payout schedule

## Tax Information

### Sales Tax

HEKKA MARKET automatically handles sales tax collection and remittance for you. You don't need to worry about calculating or collecting sales tax from buyers.

### Income Tax

As a seller, you're responsible for reporting income earned on HEKKA MARKET to your tax authorities. We provide the following documentation to help:

- **Monthly Sales Reports**: Detailed breakdown of all sales and fees
- **Annual Earnings Statement**: Summary of your yearly earnings
- **Tax Forms**: 
  - US sellers: 1099-K (if you meet the threshold requirements)
  - International sellers: Local tax documentation as required

### VAT/GST

For international sales, HEKKA MARKET handles VAT/GST collection and remittance in supported countries. You don't need to register for VAT/GST in these jurisdictions.

## Handling Refunds

### Refund Policy

HEKKA MARKET has a standard 30-day refund policy for digital products. Buyers can request refunds within 30 days of purchase if they're not satisfied with the product.

### Refund Process

1. Buyer submits a refund request
2. You receive a notification
3. You have 48 hours to respond:
   - Approve the refund
   - Deny the refund with explanation
   - If no response, the refund is automatically processed

### Impact on Your Earnings

When a refund is processed:
- The purchase amount is deducted from your balance
- If your balance is insufficient, it will be deducted from your next payout
- Platform fees are non-refundable
- Processing fees are not refunded

### Reducing Refunds

To minimize refunds:
- Provide accurate product descriptions
- Deliver high-quality products
- Respond promptly to customer inquiries
- Offer good customer support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### When will I get paid?

Payments are released according to your chosen payout schedule after the applicable holding period. For example, if you choose weekly payouts with a 7-day holding period, sales from Monday-Sunday will be paid out the following Monday.

### Why was my payout delayed?

Payouts may be delayed for several reasons:
- Account verification incomplete
- Unusual activity triggering a security review
- Bank processing delays
- Insufficient minimum balance

### How do I change my payout method?

Go to your Seller Dashboard → Payments → Payout Settings to update your payout method. Note that changing your payout method may require additional verification.

### What happens if a buyer disputes a charge?

If a buyer initiates a chargeback:
1. You'll be notified immediately
2. You'll have the opportunity to provide evidence
3. The disputed amount will be held until resolution
4. If the dispute is resolved in your favor, the funds will be released to you
5. If the dispute is resolved in the buyer's favor, the amount will be deducted from your balance

### How do I qualify for the Premium Seller program?

Once your lifetime sales on HEKKA MARKET exceed $10,000, you'll automatically be enrolled in the Premium Seller program. You'll receive an email notification when you qualify, and your reduced fee rate will apply to all future sales.

### Can I offer my own refund policy?

No, HEKKA MARKET maintains a platform-wide 30-day refund policy to ensure consistency for buyers. This policy cannot be modified by individual sellers.
