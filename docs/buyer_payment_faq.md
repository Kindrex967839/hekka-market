# HEKKA MARKET Buyer Payment FAQ

Welcome to HEKKA MARKET! This FAQ answers common questions about payments, purchases, and refunds on our platform.

## Payment Methods

### What payment methods are accepted on HEKKA MARKET?

HEKKA MARKET accepts the following payment methods:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- PayPal (where available)

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes, HEKKA MARKET never stores your credit card information. All payments are processed through Lemon Squeezy, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor with industry-standard security measures.

### Can I use multiple payment methods for a single purchase?

No, each purchase must be completed using a single payment method.

### Do you accept cryptocurrency?

Not at this time. We're evaluating cryptocurrency payment options for future implementation.

### Can I pay with my local currency?

Yes, HEKKA MARKET supports multiple currencies. The price will be displayed in your local currency, and your payment will be converted automatically if necessary.

## Purchase Process

### How do I make a purchase on HEKKA MARKET?

1. Find the digital product you want to buy
2. Click the "Buy Now" or "Add to Cart" button
3. Review your order
4. Enter your payment information
5. Complete the purchase
6. You'll receive immediate access to your digital product

### Will I receive a receipt for my purchase?

Yes, you'll receive a purchase confirmation email with a receipt immediately after your purchase is complete. You can also view your purchase history in your account dashboard.

### What happens if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, you'll see an error message with details about why the payment wasn't successful. You can try again with the same payment method or use a different one.

Common reasons for payment failures include:
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect card information
- Expired card
- Bank decline (security measures)

### Can I buy products as a gift for someone else?

Yes, you can purchase products as gifts. During checkout, you'll have the option to send the product access to a different email address.

## Digital Product Access

### How do I access my purchased products?

After completing your purchase, you'll have immediate access to your digital products. You can access them in several ways:
- Through the confirmation email (contains download links)
- From your "My Purchases" page in your account
- Via the direct access link provided after purchase

### How long do I have access to my purchased products?

Unless otherwise specified, you have lifetime access to your purchased digital products. You can download them as many times as you need from your account.

### What if I lose access to my purchased products?

If you lose access to your purchased products, you can:
1. Check your email for the original purchase confirmation
2. Log in to your HEKKA MARKET account and go to "My Purchases"
3. Contact customer support if you still can't access your products

### Can I download my purchases on multiple devices?

Yes, you can download and access your purchases on multiple devices. There are no device limitations for standard digital products.

## Pricing and Taxes

### Why am I being charged sales tax?

Sales tax is charged based on your location and local tax laws. HEKKA MARKET is required to collect and remit sales tax in many jurisdictions. The applicable tax is always displayed before you complete your purchase.

### Why does the price include VAT/GST?

For buyers in countries with Value Added Tax (VAT) or Goods and Services Tax (GST), these taxes are included in the displayed price in accordance with local regulations.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No, HEKKA MARKET does not charge any hidden fees. The price you see is the total price you'll pay, including any applicable taxes.

## Refunds and Returns

### What is HEKKA MARKET's refund policy?

HEKKA MARKET offers a 30-day refund policy for digital products. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund within 30 days of the purchase date.

### How do I request a refund?

To request a refund:
1. Log in to your HEKKA MARKET account
2. Go to "My Purchases"
3. Find the product you want a refund for
4. Click "Request Refund"
5. Provide a reason for your refund request
6. Submit your request

### How long does it take to process a refund?

Refund requests are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Once approved, the refund will be issued to your original payment method, which may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear depending on your payment provider.

### Can all products be refunded?

Most digital products on HEKKA MARKET are eligible for refunds within the 30-day window. However, some products may have specific refund limitations, which will be clearly stated on the product page before purchase.

### What if my refund request is denied?

If your refund request is denied by the seller, you'll receive an explanation. If you believe the denial was unfair, you can appeal to HEKKA MARKET support for review.

## Account and Billing Issues

### How do I update my payment information?

To update your payment information:
1. Log in to your HEKKA MARKET account
2. Go to "Account Settings"
3. Select "Payment Methods"
4. Add a new payment method or update existing ones

### I was charged twice for the same product. What should I do?

If you notice a duplicate charge:
1. Check your purchase history to confirm the duplicate charge
2. Contact HEKKA MARKET support with your order numbers
3. We'll investigate and process a refund for the duplicate charge

### How do I report unauthorized charges?

If you see charges you don't recognize:
1. Contact HEKKA MARKET support immediately
2. Provide details of the unauthorized transaction
3. Consider contacting your payment provider to dispute the charge
4. Change your account password as a precaution

## Subscriptions

### How do I cancel a subscription?

To cancel a subscription:
1. Log in to your HEKKA MARKET account
2. Go to "My Subscriptions"
3. Find the subscription you want to cancel
4. Click "Manage Subscription"
5. Select "Cancel Subscription"
6. Confirm your cancellation

### When will my subscription renew?

Your subscription renewal date is displayed in your "My Subscriptions" section. You'll receive an email reminder 3 days before your subscription is set to renew.

### What happens if my payment method expires before renewal?

If your payment method expires or becomes invalid, we'll attempt to notify you before your next renewal date. You'll need to update your payment information to continue your subscription.

## International Purchases

### Can I make purchases from any country?

Yes, HEKKA MARKET accepts purchases from most countries worldwide. However, certain products may have geographic restrictions set by the seller.

### Are there additional fees for international purchases?

There are no additional platform fees for international purchases. However, your payment provider may charge currency conversion fees or foreign transaction fees.

### Will I be charged customs or import duties?

Since HEKKA MARKET sells digital products, there are typically no customs or import duties. Digital products are generally not subject to these fees.

## Contact and Support

### How do I contact customer support about payment issues?

For payment-related issues, you can contact our support team at:
- Email: support@hekkamarket.com
- Support ticket: Log in to your account and go to "Help & Support"
- Response time: We aim to respond to all payment inquiries within 24 hours

### What information should I provide when reporting a payment issue?

When reporting a payment issue, please include:
- Your order number or transaction ID
- Date of the transaction
- Description of the issue
- Screenshots of any error messages (if applicable)
- The payment method used
